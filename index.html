Dampokh

    Ingrediants
  • Meet 1kg(depending on number of person)

  • Potato 1/2 kg

  • Three onion

  • Two Garlic

  • Foure Tomato

  • oil 1.5ml

  • Salt 2 teaspone

Method

  1. very first drop oil to the presure cooker
  2. Then put potatos on oil
  3. then put meet on potatos
  4. then put onion (dont cut onion)
  5. then put tomatos (dont cut tomatos)
  6. then put Garlic (dont cut it)
  7. then added some salt to pressure cooker (amount is mentioned above)
  8. then close the presure cooker
  9. then keep the pressure cooker on very slow amount of heat for 1 hr and 15 mins
